# Quality Test Prompts — Playful Geometric

Use these prompts to run **Law 1 (Extrapolation Test)** before publishing or after major changes.

---

## Prompt 1: Modal Dialog

> Build a fun "Are you sure?" modal with a bouncy entrance animation. Include a confetti decoration in the background and hard pop shadow on the modal card.

**Pass criteria:**
- Warm cream `#FFFDF5` background visible
- Modal has `4px 4px 0px #1E293B` hard shadow and 2px border
- Bounce easing on entrance — not ease-in-out
- Primitive shape decoration nearby

---

## Prompt 2: Pricing Toggle

> Build a monthly/annual pricing toggle switch with a pill track and sliding indicator.

**Pass criteria:**
- Pill shape (rounded-full) on track and thumb
- Hard shadow on the active state thumb
- Confetti accent colors — not muted corporate blue
- Hover translates AND changes shadow

---

## Prompt 3: Feature Comparison Table

> Build a 3-column feature comparison table for a SaaS product with checkmarks and X marks.

**Pass criteria:**
- Icons inside colored circles — never floating alone
- Table cells have chunky 2px borders
- Outfit headings + Plus Jakarta Sans body
- Section has background decoration shapes
