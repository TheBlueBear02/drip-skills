# LUXURY BOLD

Luxury Bold harmonizes minimalism with bold typography, conveying a sophisticated yet focused aesthetic. It emphasizes clarity and precision, inspired by high-end publications and architectural design, solving the problem of generic AI UIs by presenting a refined, modern aesthetic.

## WHAT YOU CAN OVERRIDE
- Primary accent color for brand emphasis
- Hero image or illustrations
- Font weights for headings

## WHAT YOU MUST NEVER OVERRIDE
- No border radius
- Consistent use of Helvetica Neue
- Flat aesthetic with absence of shadows

---

## AGENT BEHAVIOR RULES
1. Always prioritize bold typography for headings.
2. Use high contrast between text and background.
3. Ensure images remain grayscale.
4. Maintain grid-based layout with precise alignment.
5. Avoid any decorative patterns or elements.
6. Respect the monochromatic palette with neon accents.
7. Minimal animations of 200ms with `ease-in-out`.

---

## STACK REQUIREMENTS
- Tailwind CSS
- React 17+
- Framer Motion ^10.0.0
- Fonts sourced from locally included assets
