## Color Rules
1. Text is always #FFFFFF or muted #CCCCCC.
2. Accent color #EFFF00 is reserved for calls to action and key highlights.
3. Backgrounds remain #1A1A1A to provide contrast.
4. Do not introduce additional colors beyond the defined palette.
5. Ensure all text maintains high contrast with backgrounds.
