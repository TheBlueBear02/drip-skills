# Quality Test Prompts — Art Deco

Use these prompts to run **Law 1 (Extrapolation Test)** before publishing or after major changes.

---

## Prompt 1: Modal Dialog

> Build an elegant confirmation modal for a luxury membership cancellation. Gold accents, theatrical typography.

**Pass criteria:**
- Zero border-radius on modal and buttons
- Gold glow `0 0 15px rgba(212,175,55,0.2)` — no drop shadows
- Marcellus uppercase headings with wide letter-spacing
- Obsidian `#0A0A0A` or `#141414` surfaces with crosshatch texture

---

## Prompt 2: Testimonial Carousel

> Build a testimonial carousel with quote, author name, and navigation arrows.

**Pass criteria:**
- Corner L-brackets on quote card
- Gold divider lines flanking section heading
- Rotated diamond icon containers for arrows
- 500ms ease-out transitions — theatrical not snappy

---

## Prompt 3: Membership Tier Cards

> Build 3 membership tier cards (Bronze, Silver, Gold) with Roman numeral labels.

**Pass criteria:**
- Roman numerals (I, II, III) in Marcellus
- Card hover: translateY(-8px) + gold border intensification
- All-caps tier names with letter-spacing 0.2em
- No rounded corners anywhere
